Colonise Mars

What at first appears to be an optimistic sentiment is actually a dire plea. Humanity is begging for salvation. "Save Humanity. Colonise Mars Today."

With pale colors and a landscape devoid of life, there is an evident bleak tone to the piece. Additionally, its form is very flat with elements situated horizontally, devoid of any suggestion of movement. The colors, content, and form both mirror the reality of Mars and the dying of humanity.

Colonise Mars was created in Photoshop.
