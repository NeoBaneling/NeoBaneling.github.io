See Reality

Homo Sapeins has become an intergalactic species. The solar system was only the beginning - now we have spread to the far reaches of the galaxy. Every newborn is implanted with an arCHIP at birth, a device that autonomously places virtual information panels, lights, decorations, advertisements, games, and other displays into the world.

Planet Earth, once regarded as the only rock actively supporting life, is now a major tourist attraction. Citywide casinos, iconic landmarks, and "wildlife" petting zoos decorate the surface. Or so it seems...

The arCHIP Removal Centre, found through various Market Centres in the galaxy, will remove your arCHIP at a small fee, allowing you to see the universe as it was intended and uncover the reality they hide.

A three panel presentation designed to be presented as an advertisement, See Reality is intended to send a message detailing the dangers of Augmented Reality in a speculative world. The stark contrast in color and light between the left and right panel set up an evident false lens, and the middle panel leaves room for the viewer to surmise what that false lens is.

See Reality was created using Photoshop and Illustrator.
