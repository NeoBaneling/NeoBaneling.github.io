Design

The design for Shruden, as simple as it may seem, was incredibly well thought out own to the last pixel and well exhibits my design philosophy. The characters of the game can be broken down to blocks of color from which the player can derive meaning; for example, the grumpy red Blarp is easily interpreted as an enemy because of his color. The overall aesthetic of Shruden is reminiscent of old arcade games with the large, pixelated text and brightly colored title, which are designed to grab the eye's attention and pull in the player.
