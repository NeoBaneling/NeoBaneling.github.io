Creative Efforts

On the Verge of a Dream

One of the earliest drawings I can remember from my youth is a colorful Jurassic landscape featuring a Triceratops and Brachiosaurus whose skin patterns were designed with the functional intention of camouflage. Years later my artistic ability and design style have matured greatly. I am comfortable in several media, from pencil to digital work to film. My design style is analogous to Art Deco, with an emphasis on using color minimally and effectively, and a philosophy to "take something known and alter it slightly, to the point that it is on the verge of a dream". Below you should find several pieces that affirm this design style and demonstrate my ability in various media forms.

Eclipse

This digital drawing comes from a personal collection titled "Planet of Dreams", a series that explores an unknown world.

No Trash Please

While the message of this piece is quite obvious, the choices made in the design lead the eye through its composition and highlight dangerous components of the image.
