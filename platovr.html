Plato

Where anyone can be anything

Plato is a speculative virtual reality education company that "allows anyone to be anything." The brand design and guidelines were developed to reflect the ideals and focus this speculative company would have. The project was developed in Illustrator and Photoshop for the final Speculative "Self-Brand Identity" project in a Principles of Visual Design class.

{ Be inquisitive, stay positive, always strive for success, and watch out for facehuggers }
