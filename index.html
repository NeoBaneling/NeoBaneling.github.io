Programmed by Design

A Portfolio by a student who can code things and make them look good too

Welcome to my portfolio webpage! Here I showcase a few projects that I believe exemplify my skills as a programmer and a designer, and provide some interesting insights in the concept and development of these projects. I also provide "case studies", or projects that have so much depth I have given them their own page.

I have an extensive breadth and depth in programming, as I am very familiar with several languages and can use those languages for many applications. I have experience with video game programming, data analysis and visualisation, android app development, to name a few.

My design style has been developed over several years of sketches, digital art, and films, and can best be expressed as minimalism in color, akin to Art Deco. I believe that less is more, and it is often best to extrapolate from what is already existing than to include something new. A great example of this is an excerpt from my "Planet of Dreams" series which features minimal color and negative space to shape a strong image.
