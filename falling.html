Falling

From raindrops to solar rays to loss, each of these pieces of art shares a feeling of falling, whether it be physical or emotional.

The pieces are designed to mimic falling in their lines and content. Diagonal lines fall from the top left to the bottom right, vertical lines pull from top to bottom, and a figure suspended in mid air suggests an unfortunate soul plummeting to her doom.

Even the arrangement of the pieces pulls the viewer into an interactive falling experience, having to scroll down piece by piece as the background slowly fades from white to black, dropping the viewer into the abyss as they interact with the series.

The Falling series was created in Illustrator.
